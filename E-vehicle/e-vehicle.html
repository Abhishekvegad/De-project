Electric Vehicle Usage Guide

Driving Your Electric Vehicle

1. Start the vehicle by pressing the power button or turning the ignition key, depending on the model.

2. Put the vehicle in drive or select the desired driving mode (e.g., eco mode, sport mode).

3. Accelerate gently to start moving, using the accelerator pedal.

4. Drive as you would with a traditional gasoline-powered vehicle, but keep in mind the limitations of electric range and plan your route accordingly.

Charging Your Electric Vehicle

1. Plug the charging cable into your electric vehicle's charging port.

2. Connect the other end of the cable to a power source, such as a charging station or wall outlet.

3. Follow any instructions on the charging station or your vehicle's dashboard to initiate charging.

4. Wait for the vehicle to fully charge, which can take several hours depending on the battery capacity and charging speed.

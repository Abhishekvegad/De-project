About Carpooling

Carpooling, also known as car-sharing or ride-sharing, is the sharing of car journeys so that more than one person travels in a car, and prevents the need for others to have to drive to a location themselves.

Benefits of Carpooling

  • Reduces traffic congestion
  • Saves money on fuel and parking costs
  • Reduces air pollution and greenhouse gas emissions
  • Promotes social interaction
  • Reduces stress of driving

How Carpooling Works

To start carpooling, you can either join an existing carpool or create your own. You can use carpooling apps or websites to find potential carpool partners and coordinate schedules and routes.

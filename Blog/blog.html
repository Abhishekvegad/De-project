The Impact of Deforestation on Climate Change

Posted on April 20, 2024

Deforestation, the widespread clearance of forests for various purposes, significantly contributes to climate change. Trees play a crucial role in absorbing carbon dioxide, a greenhouse gas responsible for global warming. When forests are cleared, this natural carbon sink is diminished, leading to increased CO2 levels in the atmosphere.
